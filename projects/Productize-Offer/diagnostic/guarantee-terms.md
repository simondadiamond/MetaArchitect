# Guarantee Terms — AI Readiness Diagnostic

> What the better-than-risk-free guarantee means in practice — the exact mechanics, signed by the person doing the work. Attached to the booking invoice.
> Part of the AI Readiness Diagnostic kit — prices per `../pricing-pinned.md`.

If the findings memo isn't worth the fee, you get the fee back. Here is exactly how that runs — there is no fine print below this line.

1. **What triggers it.** You tell me the memo wasn't worth the fee. Any wording counts — an email saying "this wasn't worth it" is enough. No form, no justification required.

2. **The window.** 14 days from the readout date. The readout date is the day the 1-hour readout call actually happens — if the readout is rescheduled, the clock runs from when it actually happens, not from when it was first booked.

3. **No argument, no negotiation.** No rebuttal, no "can we hop on a call about it," no revised offer. You get one written confirmation, same business day.

4. **The refund.** Full amount, within 5 business days, on the same rail the payment came in on.

5. **You keep every deliverable.** The memo, the scores, the first moves — yours. Nothing is clawed back, watermarked, or replaced with a "revised" copy.

6. **The founding exchange dissolves.** No testimonial, no debrief, no intros are owed on a refunded engagement — and none will be asked for.

7. **What happens on my side.** The slot reopens, and your request is logged as product feedback, not a dispute.

Why this page exists: when you bring in an outside diagnostic, you're risking more than the fee — you're risking your name on the decision to bring me in. This guarantee moves that risk back where it belongs: on the seller.
