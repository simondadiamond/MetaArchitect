# Operating Rhythm — The $8k Motion

> Set 2026-07-06 (COO audit, plan: dazzling-purring-tarjan). Budget: ~7h/week.
> Headline metric: **ICP conversations started ≥ 2/week** (leads table), not engagement rate.
> Split: 80% of hours on the $8k motion, 20% on moat bets (FR/Loi 25, interior case studies). Reassess monthly in /weekly-review.

## Weekly

| When | What | Time |
|------|------|------|
| Daily | ICP superstar-list commenting — diagnostic one-liners on anchors' posts | 15 min |
| Mon | Publish repurposed post + reply to comments | 30 min |
| Thu | Publish second post + reply to comments | 30 min |
| Fri | `/weekly-review` — check conversations metric first | 30 min |

## Alternating weeks

- **Week A — Teardown (~2.5–3h):** `/teardown-generate` → review → publish blog → `/repurpose` into 2–3 LinkedIn candidates.
- **Week B — Outreach (~1.5–2h):** 10 DMs from the current teardown's `.outreach.md` kit to named ICP contacts whose systems resemble the target. Log replies as leads. **A teardown that hasn't been sent to its 10 people is half-spent effort.**

## Monthly (the 20% slot)

- 1 FR Loi 25 piece (~2h) — the francophone moat bet.

## Rules

1. No teardown ships without its 10 DMs.
2. Publish target stays 2 posts/week — don't raise it; raise conversations instead.
3. A lead = a two-way exchange started, not a DM sent.
4. New initiatives don't open until a current one closes (WIP cap: the goals table showed 6 open fronts on 5–10h/wk — never again).
